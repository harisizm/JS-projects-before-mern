Chili Dry

This is a recipe for making dried chili peppers.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups dried chili peppers
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon salt

Steps

  1. Soak the dried chili peppers in water for 30 minutes.
  2. Drain the water and blend the peppers into a paste.
  3. Mix the paste with salt and water to achieve the desired consistency.
