Delicious Chicken Chowmein

Description

Chowmein is a popular Chinese stir-fried noodle dish that combines tender noodles with fresh vegetables and protein. This recipe creates a flavorful and satisfying meal that's perfect for lunch or dinner. The combination of soy sauce, garlic, and fresh vegetables creates an authentic taste that will transport you straight to your favorite Chinese restaurant.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz fresh or dried chowmein noodles (or lo mein noodles)
  • 2 boneless chicken breasts, sliced thin
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, julienned
  • 2 carrots, julienned
  • 1 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the chowmein noodles according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Season the sliced chicken with salt, pepper, and cornstarch. Mix well.
  3. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Add the chicken and stir-fry for 4-5 minutes until cooked through. Remove and set aside.
  5. Add remaining oil to the same pan. Add garlic and ginger, stir-fry for 30 seconds.
  6. Add onion and bell pepper, stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until slightly softened.
  7. Add carrots and cabbage, stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes.
  8. Return the cooked chicken to the pan and mix well.
  9. Add the cooked noodles, soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil.
  10. Toss everything together for 2-3 minutes until heated through and well combined.
  11. Garnish with chopped green onions and sesame seeds if desired.
  12. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade chowmein!

Tips

  • Don't overcook the vegetables - they should remain crispy
  • You can substitute chicken with beef, pork, or tofu
  • Add bean sprouts for extra crunch
  • Adjust soy sauce according to your taste preference
